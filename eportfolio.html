CSIS 1430 - Internet and HTML Fundamentals

Course Description

Students learn to use the Internet to create Websites. Topics include basic Internet applications: HTML, XHTML, CSS, XML, and JavaScript. The Internet, with all of its social and legal implications is also be studied.

Course Reflection

This is my first course in internet, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I created my first websites in this class. It is a very fulfulling experience to create something that can be seen and used by others. I went from not knowing anything about the internet, or how it worked, to understanding basic website set-up, styling, and automation. It has helped me to think more like a computer and will continue to impact me as I take other courses and apply them to my professional career.
